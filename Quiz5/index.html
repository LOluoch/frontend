THE MUSIC OF A MOVEMENT

The 116 Clique,or better known as the Unashamed Movement was started by 6 young men who wanted a way to combine their love for hiphop and God.It consists of Lecrae,Triplee,Tedashii,Derek Minor,Andy Mineo and KB.Amisho Baraka, who was also a founder left. The group now has a recording company, Reach Records.The Unashamed Movement has become a global phenomenon among most Christians.

HIPHOP AND THE CHURCH

Initially, the group faced a lot of resistance within religious circles bacause of the association of hiphop with negative things such as drugs,gang activity and sex. However,over the years, the church has come to accept that God can use even that which was meant for evil to do good.
